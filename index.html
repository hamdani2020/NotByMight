About Us

Not By Might Company Limited is a Ghanaian engineering, procurement, and supply company that offers integrated solutions for capital efficiency, cost, and schedule certainty. With a high reputation and qualified technicians, the company specializes in electrical and electronic supplies, housing maintenance, and electrical installations.

Mission Statement

Serving our customers by adding superior value with our industry expertise, innovative systems and commitment to them

Our Vision

To provide the most reliable service with the best affordable products to customers while building of basic infrastructure of electrical requirement in our country.

Values

Our values are our cultural genetic code, the essential and enduring principles that define us as a company:

  • Uncompromising commitment to quality, health, safety and the environment
  • An open relationship with our employees based on mutual trust, respect and success
  • Transparency, accountability and discipline in our business
  • Best in class risk awareness
  • Integrity in all we do
  • Financial responsibility to our stakeholders

Behaviour

Not By Might Construction's behaviours are a set of principles that drive our day-to-day business activities. They are the backbone of our corporate culture and reinforce our mission, vision and values and ensure our motto.

Quality Delivering Certainty

For Not By Might Construction, quality is standard practice every day built into our work product. Like our vigilant approach to safety, the commitment to quality on our projects stands equally as high. All our employees and trades men are provided the training and tools to produce results. More than a program, ours is an approach focused on identifying and eliminating potentials issues together, so the quality standards achieved on each and every Not By Might project are truly Quality without Question. At Not By Might “quality” is not a single activity but a series of many linked integrated into our day-to-day commercial construction and project management processes. These are proactively planned, performed and tracked from the earliest stages of a project during preconstruction, through construction and posted final completion. By embedding quality activities into our process, we focus on building the project right the first time, not adding layers of departments and management.
